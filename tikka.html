Chicken Tikka-Masala

Pictured: Chicken Tikka Masala

This easy recipe can be brought together as fast as you desire (within just 15 minutes!) and is great for last second, low prep solutions to a weekday dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 Boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 Cup of White Rice
  • 1 Jar Tikka Masala Sauce

Steps

  1. Start by washing rice and boiling in rice cooker.
  2. Cut chicken thighs into 1/2 inch pieces and cook chicken thighs on stovetop
  3. Combine cooked chicken thighs with jar of tikka-masala sauce in pan
  4. Plate with white rice
