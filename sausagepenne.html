Sausage Penne

Pictured: Sausage Penne

This recipe is all about flexibility! No need to use sausage or penne for that matter! Simple pasta is at your fingertips.

Ingredients

  • 1 Sausage
  • 1 Cup of Penne
  • 1 Jar Pasta Sauce

Steps

  1. Begin by boiling Penne in large pot
  2. Cut open sausage and brown filling on stovetop.
  3. Combine Sausage with Pasta sauce and Penne in pan and simmer for a short period.
  4. Ready to Eat!
