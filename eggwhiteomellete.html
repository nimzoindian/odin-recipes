Egg White Omellete

Pictured: Egg White Omellete

A recipe that only improves with your skills. A simple healthy breakfast to eat every morning!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of Egg Whites
  • 1 Tsp Olive Oil
  • 1/2 Cup of Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Add oil to preheated non-stick pan and add egg whites
  2. Allow to cook for a few minutes, then add cheese and seasoning.
  3. Cover for short period for inside to cook, then fold outer edge to center on either sides as in image.
  4. Ready to Eat!
